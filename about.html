Here is more about
us.

Welcome to Low Cost Housing, where we are committed to providing an affordable and interest-free solution to the Australian housing market. Our founders Ahmad Quesada, Waseem Ghulam, and Sayed Azam share a passion for making homeownership accessible to all, and together they bring decades of experience in civil engineering, IT, and entrepreneurship. Low Cost Housing's success is due in large part to the diverse skill set and experience of our founders. Ahmad Quesada, with over 30 years of experience in civil and construction engineering, has worked on numerous high-profile projects across Sydney and Wollongong, including the Sydney Kingsfordsmith International Airport and the Sydney Olympics Dome. His expertise in research, precast development, and supervision of road and bridge construction is invaluable to our ability to deliver high-quality, affordable homes to our customers. Waseem Ghulam and Sayed Azam, both successful IT entrepreneurs, invested over three years in research and development to create a business structure that could provide a feasible pathway for prospective buyers to own their own home. Their drive and business acumen have been essential in getting Low Cost Housing off the ground and providing an innovative and affordable solution to the Australian housing market.



Our Vision.

Our vision is simple - we want to revolutionize the housing market and offer an alternative solution to those who do not want to take out a mortgage. We believe that owning a home should not be a luxury, but a feasible aspiration for everyone. By delivering a low-cost housing platform, we are giving the community the opportunity to fulfill their dream of homeownership on an interest-free basis.

At Low Cost Housing, we believe that simple living is the key to a healthy and satisfying lifestyle. We focus on simplifying the process of owning a property by removing the burden of interest and providing affordable solutions that cater to our customer's needs. We are dedicated to providing a transparent and straightforward service that puts our customers first.

Join us on our mission to make homeownership accessible to all. Contact us today to learn more about our interest-free affordable housing solutions.
